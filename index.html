Embed your menu

Add your Menu Go App menu directly to your website using a simple iframe. It stays updated automatically when you change your menu.

  1. Copy the snippet below.
  2. Paste it into your website where you want the menu to appear.
  3. Replace the URL with your menu page link.
<iframe
  src="https://menugoapp.com/menus/ENTER-YOUR-URL-HERE"
  style="width:100%; height:900px; border:0; border-radius:12px;"
  loading="lazy"
></iframe>

Tip: Adjust the height to fit your page layout.
